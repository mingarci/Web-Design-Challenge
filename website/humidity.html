Humidity

This graph illustrates the correlation between wind speed and latitude. Humidity varies among cities, even at the same Latitude. One thing to notice is that there is variance in humidity that tends to decrease at specific latitudes, such as from -20 degrees to 0 degrees latitude and at around 40 degrees to approximately 75 degrees latitude. As Latitude decreases from that range of latitudes, such clusters become rarer while common for the northern hemisphere.
