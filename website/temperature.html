Max Temperature

This graph illustrates that the closer an area is to zero degrees latitude, the higher the temperature. There is a variation of temperatures between the north and south hemispheres. Initially, it appears that the process of randomly generating latitude and longitude led to cities in the northern hemisphere. Next, however, one should consider more inhabited lands and more people in the northern hemisphere.
